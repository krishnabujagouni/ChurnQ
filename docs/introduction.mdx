## What is ChurnQ?

ChurnQ sits between your subscriber and their cancel button. When someone tries to cancel, ChurnQ intercepts that click, opens an AI-powered retention chat, and offers a personalized incentive  a discount, pause, or extension  to keep them subscribed.

When a subscriber accepts, ChurnQ automatically applies the offer to their Stripe subscription and charges you a 15% success fee on the MRR saved. You only pay when it works.

---

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscriber clicks cancel">
    Your existing cancel button is intercepted by the ChurnQ snippet (`cs.js`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Aria opens a chat">
    ChurnQ's AI retention assistant  Aria  starts a conversation, understands why they want to cancel, and offers the right incentive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscriber accepts the offer">
    The offer is applied to their Stripe subscription automatically. No manual work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You get notified">
    A save alert fires to Slack, Discord, or any webhook you've connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="ChurnQ charges 15%">
    A 15% fee on the retained MRR is charged via Stripe Connect  only on successful saves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What you need to get started

| Requirement | Why |
|---|---|
| A Stripe account | ChurnQ applies offers and charges fees via Stripe Connect |
| A website or web app | The `cs.js` snippet needs to be installed |
| A cancel button | ChurnQ intercepts it  any HTML element works |
