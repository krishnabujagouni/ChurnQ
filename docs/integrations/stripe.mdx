## Why Stripe Connect is required

ChurnShield applies discounts, pauses, and extensions directly to your subscribers' Stripe subscriptions. To do this on your behalf, it needs OAuth access to your Stripe account via Stripe Connect.

Without Stripe Connect:
- Offers are made in the chat but never applied to the subscription
- The 15% success fee cannot be charged
- Save sessions are recorded but unconfirmed

---

## Connect your Stripe account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Connections">
    Open **Dashboard → Connections** and click **Connect** next to Stripe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize ChurnShield">
    You'll be redirected to Stripe. Log in and click **Connect my Stripe account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back">
    ChurnShield saves your Stripe Connect account ID. The Stripe row shows **● Connected**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Once connected, test by going through a cancel flow and accepting an offer. Check your Stripe dashboard  the coupon or pause should appear on the subscription within seconds.
</Check>

---

## What ChurnShield does in Stripe

### Discount offers
- Creates a reusable coupon: `churnshield_ret_{pct}p_{months}m` (e.g. `churnshield_ret_25p_3m`)
- Applies it to the subscriber's active subscription
- Duration: **repeating for 3 months**, then the subscription returns to full price
- ChurnShield coupons never stack  a new save replaces the previous retention coupon

### Pause offers
- Sets `pause_collection: { behavior: "mark_uncollectible" }` on the subscription
- The subscriber is paused immediately

### Extension offers
- Adds a negative customer balance credit equal to 2 weeks of their MRR
- Applied as a credit toward their next invoice

---

## The 15% success fee

ChurnShield charges 15% of the MRR retained, per successful save. The fee is:

- **Immediate** for pause and empathy offers (confirmed right away)
- **Deferred** for discount and extension offers (confirmed after `invoice.paid` fires from Stripe)

Fees are charged automatically to your Stripe account via Connect  no manual invoices.

---

## Reconnecting

If you need to reconnect (e.g. after revoking access in Stripe), click **Reconnect →** next to the Stripe row in the Connections page. This starts a fresh OAuth flow.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The offer was accepted but nothing changed in Stripe">
    Make sure your Stripe account is connected. Go to Dashboard → Connections and check the Stripe row. If it shows "Not connected", click Connect and complete the OAuth flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see 'subscription_customer_mismatch' in session logs">
    This happens when the `subscriptionId` passed in `ChurnShield.identify()` belongs to a different customer than the `subscriberId`. Make sure both IDs are from the same Stripe customer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see 'no_active_subscription_found'">
    The subscriber has no active subscription in Stripe. ChurnShield only applies offers to `active` subscriptions. Check that the `subscriberId` is the correct Stripe customer ID (`cus_...`).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
