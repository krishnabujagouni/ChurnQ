## What you'll get

ChurnShield sends two types of alerts to Slack:

| Alert | When it fires |
|---|---|
| **Subscriber retained** | Real-time  when a subscriber accepts an offer and clicks "Keep my subscription" |
| **High-risk subscriber** | Daily  when the churn prediction job flags a subscriber with a high risk score |

---

## Before you connect

Create a dedicated Slack channel first  we recommend `#churnshield-alerts`. During the OAuth flow, Slack will ask which channel to post to. You'll select this channel.

<Tip>
  ChurnShield cannot create Slack channels automatically. Create the channel before clicking Connect.
</Tip>

---

## Connect Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Connections">
    Open **Dashboard → Connections** and click **Authorize** next to Slack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in Slack">
    You'll be redirected to Slack. Select your workspace, choose the channel (e.g. `#churnshield-alerts`), and click **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back">
    ChurnShield saves your Slack webhook URL. The Slack row shows **● Connected · #churnshield-alerts**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the alerts look like

### Subscriber retained
```
✅ Subscriber retained  YourCompany

Customer       cus_abc123
Email          user@example.com
Offer type     discount
Discount       25% off
MRR saved      $36.75
```

### High-risk subscriber
```
⚠️ High-risk subscriber detected  YourCompany

Customer       cus_xyz789
Risk score     82% (high)
Cancel attempts  2
Failed payments  1
Days inactive    18
```

---

## Disconnect Slack

Go to **Dashboard → Connections** → click **Disconnect Slack**. ChurnShield removes the webhook URL immediately. No more alerts will be sent.

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving alerts">
    Check that the Slack row shows ● Connected. If not, click Authorize and reconnect. Also check that your Slack channel is still active and the ChurnShield app has not been removed from the workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Alerts are going to the wrong channel">
    Disconnect and reconnect  during the OAuth flow, select the correct channel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
