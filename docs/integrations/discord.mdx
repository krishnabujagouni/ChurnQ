title: Discord
description: Get notified in Discord when a subscriber is saved or flagged as high-risk.
---

## What you'll get

ChurnShield sends two types of alerts to Discord as rich embeds:

| Alert | When it fires |
|---|---|
| **Subscriber retained** | Real-time  when a subscriber accepts an offer | Green embed |
| **High-risk subscriber** | Daily  when the churn prediction job flags a subscriber | Amber embed |

---

## Before you connect

Create a dedicated channel in your Discord server first  we recommend `churnshield-alerts`. During the OAuth flow, Discord will ask which server and channel to post to.

<Tip>
  ChurnShield cannot create Discord channels automatically. Create the channel before clicking Connect.
</Tip>

---

## Connect Discord

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Connections">
    Open **Dashboard → Connections** and click **Authorize** next to Discord.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in Discord">
    You'll be redirected to Discord. Select your server, choose the channel (e.g. `churnshield-alerts`), and click **Authorize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back">
    ChurnShield saves your Discord webhook URL. The Discord row shows **● Connected**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the alerts look like

### Subscriber retained (green embed)
```
✅ Subscriber retained  YourCompany
─────────────────────────────────────
Customer        cus_abc123
Email           user@example.com
Offer type      discount
Discount        25% off
MRR saved       $36.75
```

### High-risk subscriber (amber embed)
```
⚠️ High-risk subscriber  YourCompany
─────────────────────────────────────
Customer        cus_xyz789
Risk score      82% (high)
Cancel attempts 2
Failed payments 1
Days inactive   18d
```

---

## Disconnect Discord

Go to **Dashboard → Connections** → click **Disconnect Discord**. ChurnShield removes the webhook URL and no more alerts will be sent.

---

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving alerts">
    Check the Discord row shows ● Connected. If not, click Authorize and reconnect. Also verify the webhook hasn't been deleted from Discord  go to Server Settings → Integrations → Webhooks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Alerts are going to the wrong channel">
    Disconnect and reconnect  during the OAuth flow, select the correct server and channel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
