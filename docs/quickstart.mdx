title: Quickstart
description: Install ChurnShield and save your first subscriber in under 15 minutes.
---

## Step 1  Create your account

Go to [app.churnshield.ai](https://app.churnshield.ai) and sign up. Once you're in, you'll land on the dashboard.

---

## Step 2  Connect Stripe

ChurnShield needs Stripe Connect to apply discounts, pauses, and extensions to your subscribers' subscriptions.

1. Go to **Dashboard → Connections**
2. Click **Connect** next to Stripe
3. Complete the Stripe OAuth flow  you'll be redirected back automatically

<Info>
  Stripe Connect is required. ChurnShield cannot apply offers or charge the 15% success fee without it.
</Info>

---

## Step 3  Install the snippet

Go to **Dashboard → Integration** and copy your snippet tag. It looks like this:

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn.churnshield.ai/cs.js"
  data-app-id="cs_app_YOUR_APP_ID"
  defer
></script>
```

Paste it into the `<head>` of every page where your cancel button lives. Use `defer`  not `async`.

<Warning>
  Do not use `async`. The snippet must finish loading before your cancel button renders, otherwise the intercept won't fire.
</Warning>

---

## Step 4  Identify the subscriber

Before the cancel button is shown, call `ChurnShield.identify()` with the subscriber's details:

```javascript
ChurnShield.identify({
  subscriberId: "cus_abc123",          // Stripe customer ID
  subscriptionId: "sub_xyz789",        // Stripe subscription ID (recommended)
  subscriberEmail: "user@example.com", // shown in your dashboard
  subscriptionMrr: 49.00,              // monthly value in USD
  authHash: await getAuthHash("cus_abc123"), // HMAC signature  see Signing guide
});
```

<Tip>
  `subscriptionId` is recommended but not required. Without it, ChurnShield picks the customer's first active subscription.
</Tip>

---

## Step 5  Point to your cancel button

Add `data-cs-cancel` to your cancel button or link:

```html
<button data-cs-cancel>Cancel my subscription</button>
```

That's it. ChurnShield intercepts clicks on that element and opens the retention chat.

---

## Step 6  Test it

1. Open your test page in a browser
2. Click the cancel button  the ChurnShield chat should appear
3. Go through the flow and click **Keep my subscription**
4. Check **Dashboard → Recent Sessions**  you should see the session logged

<Check>
  If the chat opens and the session appears in your dashboard, you're set up correctly.
</Check>
