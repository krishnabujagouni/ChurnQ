## Overview

When a subscriber clicks cancel, ChurnShield's AI selects the most appropriate offer from your configured offer types. You can control which offer types are active, set discount amounts, and customise the copy shown on the offer page.

---

## Accessing offer settings

Go to [ChurnShield → Settings → Offers](https://app.churnshield.ai/dashboard/settings/offers).

---

## Offer types

| Offer type | What happens | Best for |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| **Discount** | ChurnShield applies a percentage or fixed-amount coupon to the subscription via Stripe | Price-sensitive subscribers |
| **Pause** | Subscription is paused for 1–3 months, then resumes automatically | Subscribers who need a break |
| **Downgrade** | Subscriber is switched to a lower-tier plan | Over-subscribed power users |
| **Free month** | One billing cycle is waived | High-LTV subscribers worth retaining at any cost |
| **Custom** | You supply the offer text; ChurnShield records the outcome but takes no automatic Stripe action | Unique cases, manual follow-up |

---

## Discount offers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the Discount offer type">
    Toggle **Discount** on in Settings → Offers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the discount amount">
    Enter a percentage (e.g. `20`) or a fixed amount (e.g. `$10`). ChurnShield will create a Stripe coupon automatically when the offer is accepted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the duration">
    Choose **once**, **3 months**, or **forever**. This maps directly to Stripe's coupon `duration` field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the headline and body copy">
    Customise the text shown on the offer page. Use `{{discount}}` as a placeholder for the amount:

    > "Before you go  how about **{{discount}} off** your next 3 months?"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  ChurnShield charges a **15% fee** on the MRR saved by each accepted discount offer. This is calculated as `0.15 × monthly_plan_amount × coupon_months`. No fee is charged for declined offers or other offer types.
</Info>

## Pause offers

Pausing a subscription stops billing temporarily without cancelling. The subscription resumes automatically after the pause period.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Pause">
    Toggle **Pause** on in Settings → Offers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose pause duration options">
    Select which durations subscribers can choose from: **1 month**, **2 months**, or **3 months**. You can enable multiple options  ChurnShield will present them as a choice.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customise copy">
    Example headline: "Need a break? Pause your subscription and come back when you're ready."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Pause requires Stripe's **subscription pause** feature. This is available on all Stripe plans but must be enabled in your Stripe Dashboard under **Billing → Settings → Subscriptions**.
</Warning>

---

## Downgrade offers

Downgrading switches the subscriber to a lower-tier plan you specify.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Downgrade">
    Toggle **Downgrade** on in Settings → Offers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the target plan">
    Choose the Stripe Price ID to downgrade to. ChurnShield will list all active prices from your connected Stripe account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customise copy">
    Example: "Our **Starter plan** at $9/month might be a better fit  interested?"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI personalisation

ChurnShield's AI uses the subscriber's cancellation reason, MRR, and subscription age to select the most appropriate offer type. You do not need to configure this  it is automatic.

| Cancellation reason | Most likely offer |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| `too_expensive` | Discount or Downgrade |
| `not_using_it` | Pause |
| `missing_features` | Custom (with manual follow-up) |
| `switching_competitor` | Discount |
| `other` | Discount (default) |

To override AI selection and force a specific offer type for all subscribers, enable **Single offer mode** in Settings → Offers.

## Offer page branding

You can customise the visual appearance of the offer page:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Logo** | Shown at the top of the offer page. Upload a PNG or SVG. |
| **Brand colour** | Used for the primary button. Defaults to `#18181b`. |
| **Company name** | Shown in the offer headline. |
| **Support email** | Shown as a fallback contact link. |

## Grace mode

Grace mode prevents cancellation even if a subscriber declines the offer. Instead of cancelling immediately, the subscription enters a **7-day grace period** during which the subscriber can reconsider.

<Warning>
  Enable grace mode only if your terms of service allow it. Forcing a billing cycle after a subscriber has explicitly declined can lead to chargebacks.
</Warning>

To enable: Settings → Offers → **Grace mode** toggle.

## Testing offers

Use a Stripe test-mode subscription to exercise the full offer flow without real billing:

1. Create a test customer and subscription in the [Stripe test dashboard](https://dashboard.stripe.com/test/subscriptions).
2. Call `/api/cancel-intent` with the test subscription ID.
3. The offer page will appear  accepting it applies a real test coupon or pause in Stripe test mode.

All test events are clearly marked `livemode: false` in Stripe and do not affect your live data.
