## Overview

When a subscriber clicks cancel, ChurnShield's AI (Aria) selects the most appropriate offer from the options you have enabled. You control which offer types are active, the discount amounts, the duration, and any custom instructions for Aria.

---

## Accessing offer settings

Go to [ChurnShield → Settings](https://app.churnshield.ai/dashboard/settings).

---

## Discount offers

Discount offers apply a percentage coupon to the subscriber's Stripe subscription. Aria starts with the lowest enabled tier and escalates to higher tiers only if the subscriber is still pushing to cancel.

### Discount tiers

Select which percentages Aria is allowed to offer: **10% off**, **25% off**, and/or **40% off**. Uncheck all three to disable price-reduction offers entirely.

<Info>
  Aria always starts with the lowest tier. She only offers higher discounts if the subscriber continues to push back — she won't lead with the maximum discount.
</Info>

### Discount duration

Choose how many months the discount applies before billing returns to full price: **1, 2, 3, 6, or 12 months**. Aria quotes this exact number in the chat.

ChurnShield creates a Stripe coupon automatically when the offer is accepted:
- Coupon ID format: `churnshield_ret_{pct}p_{months}m` (e.g. `churnshield_ret_25p_3m`)
- Duration: repeating for the selected number of months
- ChurnShield coupons never stack — a new save replaces the previous retention coupon

## Retention options

### Allow subscription pause

When enabled, Aria can offer a 1-month pause as an alternative to cancelling. The subscription resumes automatically after the pause period.

ChurnShield sets `pause_collection: { behavior: "mark_uncollectible" }` on the Stripe subscription when the offer is accepted.

### Allow free extension

When enabled, Aria can offer 1–2 weeks free before cancelling. ChurnShield applies a negative customer balance credit equal to 2 weeks of the subscriber's MRR, which is applied toward their next invoice.

---

## Plan downgrade

When enabled, Aria can offer to switch the subscriber to a cheaper plan as an alternative to cancelling.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Allow plan downgrade">
    Toggle **Allow plan downgrade** on in Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe (if not already)">
    Stripe must be connected — ChurnShield imports your active prices automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select downgrade plans">
    Use the dropdown to add one or more Stripe prices as downgrade options. Each plan shows its name and monthly amount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When accepted, ChurnShield swaps the subscription to the selected plan at the **next billing cycle** — no proration is applied.

<Warning>
  Downgrade requires Stripe Connect. The plan switch is applied at the next renewal, not immediately.
</Warning>

## Custom message for Aria

Add a custom instruction (up to 300 characters) that Aria follows verbatim in every conversation. Use this to:

- Tell Aria to mention an upcoming feature: *"Always mention that feature X is launching next month"*
- Restrict what she can discuss: *"Never mention competitors by name"*
- Adjust her tone: *"Keep responses very brief — subscribers prefer short answers"*

---

## How Aria selects an offer

Aria uses the subscriber's cancellation reason, MRR, and subscription age to pick the most appropriate offer from the types you have enabled:

| Cancellation reason | Most likely offer |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Price too high | Discount (lowest tier first) |
| Not using it | Pause |
| Needs a break | Pause or free extension |
| Switching competitor | Discount |
| Over-subscribed | Plan downgrade |
| Other | Discount (default) |

Aria will not offer something you have disabled — if only pause is enabled, she only offers a pause.

---

## The 15% success fee

ChurnShield charges 15% of the MRR retained per successful save. Fee timing by offer type:

| Offer type | When the fee is charged |
|------------|------------------------|
| Discount | After the first discounted invoice pays (`invoice.paid`) |
| Pause | Immediately on save |
| Free extension | After the next invoice pays |
| Plan downgrade | After the first invoice at the new price pays |
| Empathy (no offer) | Immediately on save |

No fee is charged if the subscriber declines.
